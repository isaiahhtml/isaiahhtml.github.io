On Calculating Player Market Value

February 3, 2025

It's hard.

NA Salary is too opaque. They're protecting it so much. All I see is the overspending from previous years.

All I have so far is minimum salary stated by a Riot Official in 2017.

Minimum salary of $75,000 annual during 2017.

Which I adjusted with US Gov's inflation calculator.

$97,473.53 in inflation data 2024.

Role multiplier based on LEC 2025 salary report by Sheep Esports.

Based in order of average LEC salary 2025 by role:

  • MID - 1.0
  • JNG - 0.8
  • BOT - 0.6
  • TOP - 0.4
  • SUP - 0.2

What I got so far:

player market value: adjusted minimum salary X age function X role multiplier

And I haven't taken into account previous year's performance which is hard to calculate too.
